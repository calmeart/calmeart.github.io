Intro

Who I am

Full-stack JavaScript/TypeScript developer with over 5 years of experience, specializing in Web3 and blockchain development. I've contributed to over 10 production applications across DeFi, fund administration, HR SaaS, loyalty platforms, e-commerce, and education — working across end-to-end onchain transactional flows, multi-network multi-wallet dApp architecture, robust backend systems, scalable and resource-efficient frontend components with real-time data layers and strategic caching, and infrastructure and deployment across Docker, VPS, and cloud services.

My expertise lies in building full-stack dApps and backend systems, primarily using TypeScript, Next.js, Node.js, Viem, Wagmi, and Solidity. With over a decade of engineering experience — including five years in civil engineering and construction project administration — I tend to anticipate issues early and approach them from the technical, user experience, and business angles at once.

I'm resourceful, can find multiple ways to solve a problem, and can take a concept and deliver it as a working feature. I use AI daily in roughly 80% of my coding workflow — agents for automated bug detection, code scanning, and merge request analysis — but I never ship code without personal review. AI accelerates me; it doesn't replace judgment.
